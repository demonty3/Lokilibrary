## The headline questions

After walking the share flow with someone real:

1. **Did your friend actually click the link?** Sharing in DMs vs Discord
   vs Twitter — which channel produced a click? If the answer is "I
   shared and nobody clicked," that's the signal the share artifact
   isn't doing its job. Fix the share before Phase 6.
2. **What did they say when they opened it?** If the reaction is "this
   is cool" → the surface works. If it's "I don't understand what I'm
   looking at" → the view-only mode needs more onboarding (a header
   over the canvas explaining whose world this is, maybe a "tour" of
   the metaphor).
3. **Did you re-share the same person's world?** The reproducibility
   contract (same profile → same world) is what makes shares feel
   solid vs. ephemeral. Did you visit your own share URL on a
   different device? Did everything match?
4. **Visually, does it cohere?** Terrain + paths + scatter on top of
   procedural-primitive archetypes is a lot of visual layers — does
   the seaside scene read as "a place" or as "polygons with extra
   steps"? If the latter, the Meshy hero-asset pass (still outstanding
   from Phase 1.7) is genuinely the next bottleneck.
